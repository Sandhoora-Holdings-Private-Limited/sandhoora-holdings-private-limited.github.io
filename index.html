About Company

Sandhoora Holdings has been established in year 2015 as a property development and building construction company in Sri Lanka. Our traditional business model is based on the accomplishments of properties in the real estate markets in Sri Lanka.

Our team at Sandhoora Holdings encompasses specialists with extensive experience in the field of building construction in Sri Lanka.

Since our establishment, the Sandhoora Holding team have delivered many more residential housing, hotels and factory projects across the country. The increasingly large number of returning client base is a testament to the quality of our services and our dedication to exceeding the client expectations.

At Sandhoora Holding, we utilize cost effective methods to serve our customers. We offer innovative and resilient engineering solutions for our clients to meet their expectations.

Sandhoora Holdings has identified requirement of introducing personalized furniture for their clients. Based on these findings Sandhoora Holdings has diversified their business to manufacturing of personalized furniture and providing of interior designing.

While in the building construction field we have identified the lack of quality products for the right price in the local market. Therefore company has started looking for overseas sources to fill this gap. Accordingly now we are successfully importing some of the identified building accessories and introducing these products directly to construction industry in Sri Lanka.
