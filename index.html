About Company

Sandhoora Holdings has been established in the year 2015 as a property development and building construction company in Sri Lanka.

Our team at Sandhoora Holdings encompasses specialists with extensive experience in the field of building construction in Sri Lanka. Since our establishment, the Sandhoora Holding team have delivered many more residential housing, office buildings, hotels and factory projects across the country. The increasingly large number of returning client base is a testament to the quality of our services and our dedication to exceed client expectations. At Sandhoora Holding, we utilize cost-effective methods to serve our customers. We offer innovative and resilient engineering solutions for our clients to meet their expectations.

Businesses

Sandhoora Homes involves in Building Construction and property development as well as consultation for clients those who want personalized services in construction. The company undertakes all kinds of construction work such as new building construction for residential houses, apartments, hotels, commercial & office buildings, hospitals, shopping complex etc. We also specialize in remodelling and renovations for existing buildings and houses. Also, we extend our services as follows

  • Provide architectural drawings in details. The services of our highly-talented panel of architects (in the absence of an architect of your choice).
  • Assistance to obtain statutory approvals. Survey of the land, Title clearance, Approval of the building plan and the land plan. Coordination of all documentary work for you from the moment you come to us, up till the time, the completed house/building is handed over to you.
  • Assistance to obtain financial facilities. If you need monetary assistance, we would also handle these details, with the least amount of hassle to you. On your behalf, we would coordinate with the banks to get the best deal for you.
  • Utilisation of approved technical and engineering professionals. Use of ISO certified systems and materials for long endurance.

The intent of our designs are to take advantage of the home’s classic architecture, bring in additional natural light and utilize a combination of vintage and contemporary furnishings and accessories. We always use your special instructions as our basic guidelines for designing.

Buying a land to build your own home is a unique experience. Having established Sandhoora Lands we commit to offer livable spaces in major cities. Our expertise in site selection and constructive blocking out will help you to find an ideal living space.


Our experience in local property market has allowed us to find and provide you with properties for a competitive price for it’s value. Buying a land from us will be a future investment for you as well.
